MOIN-MOIN

The african dish moin-moin is a West-African dish mostly consumed by the YORUBA TRIBE.The main indegredient in this dish is Beans

INGREDIENTS

  • 750g of beans
  • 5 tablespoon ground crayfish
  • 4 big stock cups
  • pepper
  • 3 tatashe peppers
  • 2 big onion
  • 20cl vegetable oil
  • 2 litres of cool or warm water
  • salt
  • eggs

PROCEDURES

  1. Cut the eggs into small pieces
  2. Wash of the coat pf the soaked beans
  3. Wash the tatashe peppers and remove seeds,cut onions
  4. Blend the washed beans till it smooth
  5. Blend the pepper,crayfish and others together
  6. Mix them up in a bowl and stir properly
  7. Add vegetable oil
  8. Add salt to taste
  9. Put water in stove
  10. The put the moin moin in a container of your choice
  11. Then boil till it hard
